What we're about

We censor our names.
Never our ingredients.

Break of Dawn began in a home kitchen with a simple, stubborn idea: plant-based food should taste like a treat, not a compromise.

Everything is made in small batches from whole ingredients — soaked almonds, cold-pressed by hand, cultured slowly, sweetened with dates. No dairy, no preservatives, nothing you can't pronounce. Just genuinely good s***, the kind you'd make yourself if you had the time.
