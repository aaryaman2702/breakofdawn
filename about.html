Our Story

Made before the world wakes up.

A home kitchen, a stubborn belief, and a lot of soaked almonds. This is how Break of Dawn began.

How it started

one woman, one blender, one very early morning.

In 2015, Pardita turned vegan — and quickly found that the hard part was staying vegan. Fresh, dairy-free food simply wasn't accessible, and what sat on the shelves was full of preservatives and stabilisers.

So in June 2017 she founded Break of Dawn with a single batch of fresh, preservative-free almond milk — delivered chilled to doorsteps at the crack of dawn. A former diamond grader, she brought the same eye for quality to food. Today an all-women team makes the whole range from scratch, and the vegan feta — now voted India's No.1 plant-based cheese — has become the most talked-about product in the country.

why "break of dawn"?

the first light is the honest one.

Dawn is when everything resets. It's the freshest hour — before shortcuts, before compromise, when milk is at its creamiest and the day still holds its promise. We bottle that moment. Our sunrise mark isn't decoration; it's the whole idea, drawn in three arcs.

The little *** thing

We censor the names.
Never the label.

Almond m***. Vegan ch***. Good s***. It started as an in-joke and became our signature — a wink that says we don't take ourselves too seriously, even if we take the food very seriously indeed.

Flip any product over and there's nothing hidden: whole ingredients, plainly listed, nothing you can't pronounce. The only thing we'll ever bleep out is the punchline.
