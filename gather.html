a place to host, to teach, to create. and to gather.

Eight years ago, Break of Dawn began as a small passion project — fresh almond milk, made in a home kitchen, rooted in mindful mornings and the joy of making things from scratch.

From a handful of customers it grew into a community that cared about what goes into their bodies. Today we open the doors to a thoughtfully designed space — for movement and stillness, for sound and silence. We built it with the same love that goes into everything we make. And now, we're inviting you to make it your own.

engage

let your ideas stretch, your breath deepen.

We host mindful mornings, thought-provoking talks, and creative workshops — spaces where you can engage with both self and community.

Think yoga mats, open notebooks, and wide-eyed conversations.

connect

where strangers become collaborators.

Dinner tables turn into meeting rooms. Co-working over coffee, sharing stories over dinner, brainstorming over wine — a space designed for real connection.

This is where ideas — and people — find their people.

savour

slow down, and savour every moment.

From the aroma of freshly baked granola and cheese to the joy of plating your own creation — culinary workshops, pop-up tastings, and our most delicious offerings.

Cheese, conversation, and community — served fresh.
